FCG Church

Welcome to First Century Gospel Church, a vibrant and welcoming community dedicated to living out the teachings of Jesus Christ. Our mission is to love God, love others, and make disciples. We are committed to spiritual growth, heartfelt worship, and compassionate service. Whether you are seeking a place to deepen your faith, connect with others, or serve the community, you will find a home here. Join us as we journey together in faith, guided by the Word of God and empowered by the Holy Spirit.

Our Purpose.

Our purpose at First Century Gospel is to glorify God by living out the teachings of Jesus Christ. We aim to be a beacon of hope and love in our community, offering spiritual guidance, support, and encouragement to all who seek it. Through worship, fellowship, and service, we strive to reflect God's love and grace in everything we do. We believe in nurturing a welcoming and inclusive environment where everyone feels valued and loved. Our church is a place where faith meets action, and where lives are transformed by the power of the Gospel.

Our Mission.

Our mission is to love God, love others, and make disciples. We are dedicated to fostering a community where individuals can grow in their faith, connect with one another, and serve those in need. By sharing the Gospel, nurturing spiritual growth, and reaching out with compassion, we seek to transform lives and make a lasting impact in our world. Our mission drives us to engage with our local and global communities, addressing spiritual and practical needs with Christ-like love. We aim to empower individuals to discover their God-given purpose and live out their faith boldly and authentically

Our Values.

Identity in Christ

Our identity is found in Christ alone. We believe that through His sacrifice, we are made new and called to live as His children. Embracing our identity in Christ means understanding and accepting that we are loved, redeemed, and called to a purpose greater than ourselves. This foundational truth shapes our lives, guiding our actions, decisions, and interactions with others.

Church Is Family

At First Century Gospel, we see the church as a family where everyone belongs. We strive to create an atmosphere of love, acceptance, and support, where relationships are nurtured and each person is valued. Our community is built on mutual respect and care, reflecting the unity and fellowship that God desires for His people. Together, we share in joys, bear burdens, and grow in faith, ensuring no one walks alone.

Kingdom Culture

We are committed to cultivating a Kingdom culture that reflects the values and principles of God's Kingdom. This means living out the teachings of Jesus in every aspect of our lives, promoting justice, mercy, and humility. By prioritizing God's will over our own, we seek to influence our community and beyond with the transformative power of the Gospel, creating a world that honors God's righteousness and love.

Spirit Led Life

We believe in living a Spirit-led life, guided by the Holy Spirit in all we do. This means being open to His leading, listening for His voice, and allowing Him to work through us to accomplish God's purposes. By relying on the Holy Spirit, we are empowered to live out our faith boldly, to serve others selflessly, and to navigate life's challenges with wisdom and grace. Our dependence on the Spirit brings renewal, strength, and direction to our everyday lives.


What We Believe.

Maxime Quia Aut Dolorem.

At First Century Gospel, we believe in the transformative power of faith in Jesus Christ. We uphold the Holy Bible as the inspired and infallible Word of God, serving as our guide in all matters of faith and practice. Our faith is rooted in the belief in the Holy Trinity- God the Father, Jesus Christ the Son, and the Holy Spirit. We believe in the power of prayer, the importance of fellowship, and the necessity of spreading the Gospel to all corners of the earth. Our mission is to love God, love others, and make disciples, living out our faith through acts of service, compassion, and community

Neque Qui Veritatis.

At First Century Gospel Church, our core beliefs are grounded in the teachings of Jesus Christ and the Holy Scriptures. We affirm the sovereignty of God the Father, the saving grace of Jesus Christ, and the empowering presence of the Holy Spirit. We believe that salvation is a gift of God's grace through faith in Jesus Christ, and that the Bible is the ultimate authority for Christian living. Our community is committed to worship, discipleship, and evangelism, striving to reflect God's love through our words and actions. We seek to be a beacon of hope and light in our community, dedicated to fostering spiritual growth and serving others in love.
